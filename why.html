What shall we eat today?

 

Why this foodiewheel di?

As a young couple we were often perplexed by the decision of what to eat at every meal. Should it be a scrumptious dosa? fluffy idlis? a cheesy omlette? a sinful buttery paratha? or a healthy poha?

We often haggled with each other to make a choice and usually resorted to randomly making choices, based on convenience and the ease of preparing meal items. Yet every morning, our day started with the question 'What shall we eat today?'

The "foodiewheel" is a concept we conjured up to make choosing a meal fun and interactive.

Over time, we hope the foodiewheel grows up to become more brainy and brawny, and a lot more useful and fun. Give us your feedback and suggestions at foodies@foodiewheel.in or on twitter at @foodiewheel.

May the foodiewheel be with you.
